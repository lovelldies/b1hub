Here's how you can control your B.One Hub via the Line Messenger ChatBot:

  • Turning connected devices ON/OFF
  • IR remote control
  • Setting the security status of your home to "Arm" "Disarm" or "In-house"
  • Performing actions that have already been created in the B.One app
